Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

TripBud360 ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TripBud360 mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and name when you register
  • Location data: your device location when using the app, to discover nearby activities and points of interest
  • Trip data: destinations, saved places, itineraries, and preferences you create
  • Usage data: app interactions and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the TripBud360 service
  • To discover nearby activities and points of interest based on your location
  • To generate personalized AI travel itineraries
  • To sync your data across devices via Supabase
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Google Places API – nearby activity and location data (developers.google.com)
  • Anthropic Claude API – AI-powered itinerary generation (anthropic.com)
  • Affiliate booking partners – KKday, WeGoTrip, GoCity, and others; links may redirect to their platforms for bookings

4. Location Data

TripBud360 requests access to your device location solely to show nearby activities and points of interest. Location data is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of your session.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account and trip data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

TripBud360 is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
